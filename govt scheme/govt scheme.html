Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about government schemes and application process.

How do I check if I am eligible for a scheme?
You can check eligibility by entering your details in the scheme eligibility checker available on the portal.
What documents do I need to apply for schemes?
Required documents typically include ID proof, address proof, and income certificate. Specific schemes may need additional documents.
How long does the application process take?
The application process duration varies by scheme but generally takes 2–4 weeks for review and approval.
Can I apply for multiple schemes at once?
Yes, you can apply for multiple schemes provided you meet their eligibility requirements.
What should I do if my application is rejected?
If your application is rejected, review the reason and reapply with the correct details or documents.
